My Heroes

Invoker

Invoker is a ranged intelligence hero who is very difficult to master. He is unique in that he possesses a total of 14 abilities in his arsenal; three of them - Quas, Wex, and Exort - are reagents and one is his special ultimate Invoke.

Timbersaw

Timbersaw is a melee strength hero that excels in dealing damage and causing destruction in large areas. His effectiveness is improved by having trees around him, as they can amplify the damage of his spells and allow him to escape easily.

Faceless Void

Darkterror the Faceless Void is a melee agility hero generally played as an off-laner or carry. He becomes a terrifyingly powerful hero capable of destroying entire enemy teams in seconds. Chronosphere is the ability to trap and stop time.
